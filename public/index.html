1. Log In

Before using the deck builder or saving personal decks, enter your name in the login box at the top of the page.

  • Your name is attached to your submissions.
  • Your name is used when displaying your decks in lists.
  • No account, password, or registration is required.

2. Build Your Deck

The deck builder allows you to visually construct a Clash Royale deck using the full card roster.

  • Click any slot to select where a card will be placed.
  • Click a card from the card list to insert it.
  • Evo cards are automatically restricted to the first two slots.
  • Champion and Hero cards are restricted to their dedicated slots.
  • The system prevents illegal deck compositions in real time.
  • The average elixir cost is calculated instantly.

This ensures that all submitted decks follow actual in-game rules without requiring the user to manually check restrictions.

4. Submit a Deck for AI Review

To send your deck to the akomiya AI review system, switch to Request Review mode.

If your deck is selected, it may appear in a future akomiya video where AI evaluates its strengths, weaknesses, matchup potential, and overall design logic.
