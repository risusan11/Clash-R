Here is a simple overview of the main features and how to use them effectively.

1. Log In

Before using the builder or saving decks, enter your name in the login box at the top of the page.

  • Your name is attached to your deck submissions.
  • It is also used when displaying your decks inside lists.
  • No account or password is required ‚Äî everything is stored locally in your browser, except when you submit a deck for review.

2. Build Your Deck

The deck builder lets you create a Clash Royale deck visually, using the exact cards from the game.

  1. Click a slot to select where you want to place a card.
  2. Click a card from the card list to insert it into that slot.
  3. The builder automatically enforces:
    • Evo cards can only be placed in the first two slots.
    • Champion / Hero cards can only be placed in the next two slots.
    • You cannot accidentally add more heroes than allowed.
  4. The average elixir of your current deck is calculated and displayed in real time.

This system helps you build legal, well-structured decks quickly without manual checking.

Think of it as your own personal notebook of Clash Royale decks, integrated directly into the builder.

4. Submit a Deck for AI Review

To send your deck to the AI so it can be used in future akomiya videos, switch to Request Review mode.

  1. Make sure all 8 slots of your deck are filled.
  2. Enter the name you want displayed (or use your login name).
  3. Click Submit to send the deck.

When you submit a deck in this mode:

  • The deck is uploaded to the shared server.
  • It becomes part of the pool of decks that AI may review.
  • Your name can be shown in videos when your deck is selected.

This is the main way for viewers to participate and potentially see their deck analyzed in a video.
